HOW IT WORKS

9-Stage Security Pipeline

Every agent action passes through these stages sequentially in real time.

01

MCP Intercept

Capture raw outgoing tool call payloads at the protocol transport layer.

02

Intent Analysis

Classify prompt objectives: benign, suspicious, or malicious.

03

Injection Detection

Regex signatures & vector-space scanning for override patterns.

04

Behavior Profiling

Anomaly scoring by comparing tool args against agent baselines.

05

Risk Scoring

Weighted multi-factor risk computation across 6 dimensions.

06

Trust Engine

Dynamic trust scores that decay on violations and recover over time.

07

Policy Engine

Configurable rulesets with conditions, priorities, and automated actions.

08

Human Approval

Pipeline halts for analyst signature on critical escalation operations.

09

Execution Decision

Binary verdict: forward approved calls or block and log violations.
